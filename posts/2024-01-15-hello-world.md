Every blog starts somewhere. Mine starts here.

I've been meaning to write publicly for years — notes, observations, things I've learned the hard way. The usual friction always got in the way: the perfect platform, the perfect design, the perfect first post.

So here's an imperfect first post on a simple platform, and that's fine.

## What this is

A place to think out loud. Mostly technical, sometimes not. I'll write about things I'm building, problems I've solved, and ideas I haven't fully worked out yet.

No promises on frequency. No newsletter. Just a place where things live.

## What this isn't

A personal brand. A content strategy. A growth hack.

If something here is useful to you, I'm glad. That's enough.
